01 — Color

A warm, editorial palette

Warm neutrals as canvas, burnished gold as the single precious accent, deep teal as the quiet counterpoint. Every text/surface pair below carries its approximate contrast ratio.

Rule of gold — the accent appears once per composition: a number, a word in italic, a ring around the avatar. Never as a fill for large areas.

03 — Glass

The signature surface

One glass recipe, two tints. Dark glass is the universal default — it reads on any footage. Light glass is reserved for compositions you control (title cards, end cards on light scenes). Recipe: blur 18px + saturate 140%, radius 20px, 1px light border, soft long shadow.

05 — Motion

Calm, precise, editorial

Nothing bounces. Glass arrives like it condenses out of the air; type settles rather than lands.

Glass panels

Fade + blur-in (blur 30→18px) + rise 16px · 420ms ease-out. Exit: fade + sink 10px · 300ms ease-in.

Titles

Opacity 0→1 with letter-tracking settle (+.04em→0) · 500ms ease-out. Italic accent word arrives 120ms late.

Step numbers

Count-up flip or simple fade · 350ms. Gold shimmer sweep allowed once per video, on the final card.

Sofia frame

Scales 0.94→1 with fade on entry · 400ms ease-out. Never moves during a scene; may swap corners between scenes with a cut, not a slide.

Captions

Per-line fade · 220ms, no slide. Highlight words tinted gold-bright, no animation.

Tool chips

Stagger in 80ms apart, rise 10px · 320ms ease-out each.
